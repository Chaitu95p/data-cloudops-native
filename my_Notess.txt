PySpark Compression codecs when writing data in different formats (CSV, JSON, TEXT, Parquet):

csv, json, text:

(none, bzip2, gzip, lz4, snappy, deflate)
(none, bzip2, gzip, lz4, snappy, deflate)
(none, bzip2, gzip, lz4, snappy, deflate)

parquet:

(none, uncompressed, snappy, gzip, lzo, brotli, lz4, zstd)
